Sakura

The cherry blossom, known as 'sakura' in Japanese, is an iconic and highly symbolic flower in Japanese culture. These beautiful flowers are an integral part of spring in Japan, and their blooming is celebrated throughout the country with hanami festivals, which are events for observing and enjoying the beauty of sakura in full bloom.

Cherry blossoms have been a source of inspiration in Japanese poetry and literature for centuries.

Hanami Festivals

During the cherry blossom season, parks and gardens are filled with people enjoying picnics, music, dancing, and the contemplation of the flowers. Hanami festivals are joyful and festive events where people gather to appreciate the beauty of the cherry blossoms.
